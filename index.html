More About Me


Hey there! I'm Lian, a self-directed full stack software developer with a passion for building web apps, coding video games, and teaching.

I've gained extensive experience through personal projects, volunteer work, and freelancing, constantly pushing the boundaries of my skills and knowledge.

I've worked on a wide range of projects, from dynamic web applications to 3D and 2D video games. My volunteer work at The Working Centre involved refurbishing and repairing computers, solidifying my foundation in both hardware and software. Additionally, I've excelled in projects encompassing full stack development, data analysis, and electronics/PCB design, highlighting my dedication and diverse skill set.

I'm committed to using technology for social good. I've contributed to open-source projects that provide software solutions to nonprofits, demonstrating my ability to create impactful technology. I've also mentored middle schoolers and nonbinary students in coding and taught computer science fundamentals to high schoolers from Myanmar through the Chinland Defence program.

In my free time, I enjoy playing soccer, playing chess, tinkering with electronics, working out, traveling, and learning about new technology fields, from cybersecurity to artificial intelligence and IoT. I'm excited about the future and eager to apply my skills and passion to new opportunities. Check out my portfolio for more details on my projects and experiences!
