## Finishing project someone assign you.
- From stake holder, clients, or assignments.
- Unclear requirement, trying to see their perspective.
- Takes time to understand the whole requirement.
- Hard to disects and making things smaller.
- Maybe we start with smaller part of the project.
- We have to understand first then implement. That takes time.

## Finishing a project you started
- You have a clear understanding of what you get it done.
- There is a clear path and you know what the end product.
- It might not be the best methods or pratices to finish.
- But you can the job done and feel proud.
- This can be good for beginner but for learning advanced knowledge, it is not good.

## Over all
- we can combine the reward and understanding of these mindsets.
- Help you feel special and give you a sense of pride in learning more
- Advanced and harder subjects to learn.
